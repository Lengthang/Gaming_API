Game Play

GOP STOP AUTO – This is a dynamic first-person shooter in which you will exterminate hordes of evil memes. You appear in a large, open for exploration arena and in fact you just need to quickly get to your car to get out of here, but you will not be able to open the door to the garage until you destroy 30 opponents. Evil memes are everywhere. They are thirsty for your blood. They will pursue you wherever you go. Use a wide arsenal of firearms to get rid of these annoying entities from the lower Internet. Get diamonds for destroying memes and open new levels!
